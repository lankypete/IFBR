About Us

Our home of Port Alberni is just a short scenic trip from either Vancouver or Victoria and is in the heart of some of the best sport fishing waters anywhere in the world. In a contest held in 2010 by The World Fishing Network, Port Alberni was voted Canada’s Ultimate Fishing Town by fisherman from across the country. We have a passion for fishing and the West Coast and we want to share the spectacular coast lines and mountain scenery that the Alberni Inlet and surrounding area has to offer. Being on the water here has a special feeling like nowhere else and will create memories for friends and family that will last a lifetime. Our vessel and gear is new, reliable and “fish tested” to help make your West Coast experience the best it can be.
